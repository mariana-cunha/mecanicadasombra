MECÂNICA DA SOMBRA (2018)

Drama. 15 min.

plot

After a change that came later than planned, Moacir (Alberto Pereira Jr.) embarks on a search for answers and new perspectives. By finding a reality of non-belonging, besides the difficulty of adaptation and the internal struggles, the son of pain begins to question some questions involving the relation with family, the roots and the space and time in which he lives. Moacir is confused and forced to understand these new reflections about himself and the environment that surrounds him in this process of self-knowledge.
